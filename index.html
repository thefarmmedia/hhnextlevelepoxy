How It Works

Our Proven 5-Step Process

Every install follows the same meticulous process. No shortcuts. No skipped steps. That's why our floors last 15+ years.

1

Diamond Grinding

We mechanically grind every slab — removing coatings, oils, and weak surface paste to create a permanent bond profile.

2

Crack Repair

Every crack and control joint is filled and stabilized before coating — because a crack in concrete becomes a crack in your floor if you skip this.

3

Primer Coat

Specialized primer applied to the prepared slab for maximum adhesion between the concrete and the coating system.

4

Base Coat & Flake

Industrial-grade epoxy base coat applied, then decorative flake broadcast at full coverage for texture and your custom look.

5

Polyaspartic Topcoat

UV-stable, chemical-resistant topcoat locks it all in. Walk-on in 24 hrs, drive-on in 48. Gorgeous and maintenance-free for 15 years.

Who We Are

Two Brothers. One Standard. No Shortcuts.

HH Next-Level Epoxy was founded by brothers Kellen and Kent Hendrix out of a simple frustration: too many floor coating companies in the Richmond/Kearney service area were taking shortcuts on prep, using inferior materials, and delivering floors that looked fine on day one but failed within a few years. Kellen and Kent decided to build something different — a company where the prep is never optional and the warranty is never hollow.

Our Promise

The HH Difference Comes Down to One Thing: Prep

Most epoxy floors across Richmond, Kearney and nearby Missouri communities fail not because of the coating — but because the installer skipped or rushed the surface preparation. diamond grinding isn’t a suggestion or an optional upgrade at HH Next-Level Epoxy. It’s the first step on every single project, full stop.

That’s why we can offer a 15-year written warranty. Not because we’re optimistic — because we know the prep was done right.

What Makes Us Different from Other KC Epoxy Companies

  • Diamond grinding on every project — no acid etching, no shortcuts, no exceptions
  • 100% solid epoxy base coat — not the water-based products sold at home improvement stores
  • UV-stable polyaspartic topcoat — won’t yellow, won’t chalk, won’t fade under Kansas City sun
  • 15-year written warranty — backed by the people who did the work, not a faceless corporation
  • Locally owned — when you call, you talk to Kellen or Kent, not a call center
